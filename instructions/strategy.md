# Career Strategy: Platform Depth + AI Systems Leverage

## Purpose

This project exists to accelerate growth toward **senior, staff, and principal-level engineering roles** by focusing on *leverage*, not reinvention.

The strategy is intentionally designed to:
- Preserve platform and cloud engineering as the primary identity
- Add AI as an operational system capability
- Signal senior-level judgment, ownership, and trustworthiness

This is not an AI pivot.
This is a **platform engineer absorbing AI responsibly**.

---

## The Three Lanes

### Lane 1: Platform & Cloud Engineering (Primary Identity)

This lane is non-negotiable.

Focus:
- Kubernetes, Terraform, CI/CD, GitOps
- Reliability, observability, incident response
- Cost control and scaling under constraints
- Internal platforms and developer experience
- Security and compliance as engineering problems

Success looks like:
- Owning production systems
- Designing guardrails, not just pipelines
- Reducing risk while enabling velocity

This lane anchors employability and compensation.

---

### Lane 2: AI Systems (Additive Capability)

AI is treated as:
- A probabilistic dependency
- A cost center
- A reliability risk
- A security and compliance surface

Focus:
- LLM integration into real systems
- RAG pipelines with citations and fallbacks
- Human-in-the-loop workflows
- Prompt versioning and evaluation
- Cost limits, latency budgets, and quotas
- Observability and rollback strategies

AI is **never the product**.
AI supports platform outcomes.

---

### Lane 3: Senior Signal & Narrative

This lane determines how work is perceived.

Focus:
- Clear documentation
- Architecture diagrams
- Tradeoff explanations
- Failure modes and mitigations
- Decision-making under constraints

All work is framed as:
- Problems solved
- Risks mitigated
- Systems owned
- Outcomes delivered

---

## Target Roles This Strategy Supports

- Senior Platform Engineer
- Site Reliability Engineer
- Infrastructure Engineer
- AI Platform Engineer
- Applied AI Systems Engineer
- Staff-level Platform roles

Avoid roles focused on:
- Pure research
- Model training
- Data science without ownership

---

## Portfolio Rules

Only publish work that:
- Assumes real users
- Has failure modes
- Includes monitoring and rollback
- Demonstrates judgment

Toy projects remain private unless explicitly labeled.

---

## Quarterly Focus Model

Each quarter should:
- Advance Lane 1 measurably
- Introduce **one** Lane 2 capability
- Improve Lane 3 clarity

No quarter is allowed to stall entirely.

---

## North Star

The goal is to become the engineer who can say:

"I’ve shipped AI-powered systems. I know where they break.  
And I know how to keep them safe, reliable, and affordable in production."
