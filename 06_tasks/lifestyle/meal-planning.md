# Weekly Meal Planning

## What This Task Does

Helps you create a practical, balanced meal plan for the week that fits your schedule, preferences, and budget.

## When to Use This

- You want to eat healthier but struggle with daily decisions
- You want to reduce food waste and save money
- You need meals that fit your schedule (busy weeknights, meal prep days, etc.)
- You want variety without the mental load of deciding what to cook

## Instructions

Copy the prompt below and paste it into your AI assistant. Then provide the information requested.

### The Prompt

```
I need help creating a weekly meal plan. Please help me plan 7 days of meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner) that are practical and fit my life.

Here's what you need to know about me:

**My situation:**
- [Tell me about your schedule: busy weekdays? work from home? kids? etc.]
- [How much time do you have for cooking on weekdays vs weekends?]
- [Do you meal prep? If so, which days?]

**My preferences:**
- [Dietary restrictions or preferences: vegetarian, allergies, etc.]
- [Foods I love: pasta, Mexican, Asian, etc.]
- [Foods I avoid: spicy, seafood, etc.]
- [Cooking skill level: beginner, intermediate, advanced]

**My constraints:**
- [Budget considerations: trying to save money, willing to spend more, etc.]
- [Kitchen equipment: basic, well-equipped, etc.]
- [How many people are you cooking for?]

**My goals:**
- [What are you trying to achieve? Healthier eating, save time, save money, try new recipes, etc.]

Please create a meal plan that:
1. Fits my schedule and cooking time
2. Includes a shopping list organized by store section
3. Suggests which meals can be prepped ahead
4. Balances variety with practicality
5. Stays within my constraints

For each meal, include:
- The dish name
- Why it fits my schedule/preferences
- Any prep notes (make ahead, quick, etc.)
```

### What to Provide

Fill in the bracketed sections `[like this]` with your specific information. Be honest about your schedule and preferences—this helps create a plan you'll actually follow.

### What You'll Get

- A 7-day meal plan with breakfast, lunch, and dinner
- A shopping list organized by store section (produce, meat, dairy, etc.)
- Prep notes showing what can be made ahead
- Explanations of why each meal fits your situation

## Example

**Input:**
```
My situation:
- Work from home, busy weekdays, more time on weekends
- Can cook 30-45 min on weekdays, 1-2 hours on weekends
- Meal prep on Sundays

My preferences:
- No dietary restrictions
- Love Italian, Mexican, and comfort food
- Avoid very spicy food
- Intermediate cooking level

My constraints:
- Cooking for 2 people
- Trying to save money
- Basic kitchen equipment

My goals:
- Eat healthier, save time during the week, reduce food waste
```

**Output:**
The AI will create a personalized meal plan with specific dishes, a shopping list, and prep notes tailored to your situation.

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific about your schedule** — "I have 20 minutes on weeknights" is better than "I'm busy"
2. **Mention leftovers** — If you're okay eating the same thing twice, say so (saves time and money)
3. **Include your actual preferences** — Don't say you like healthy food if you really want comfort food (the plan won't work)
4. **Be honest about cooking skill** — Beginner plans use simpler recipes
5. **Ask for modifications** — If the first plan doesn't work, ask for adjustments

## Common Variations

- **Budget-focused:** Add "I'm trying to spend under $X per week on groceries"
- **Health-focused:** Add "I'm trying to eat more vegetables and less processed food"
- **Time-crunched:** Add "I need meals that take 15 minutes or less on weekdays"
- **Family-friendly:** Add "I need meals that kids will eat" or "I'm cooking for picky eaters"

## When to Suggest a Persona

This task works great on its own. However, if you want more strategic thinking about nutrition, meal prep systems, or long-term eating patterns, you might benefit from the **Product Thinker** persona for systematic planning or the **Finance Dragon** persona for budget optimization.
