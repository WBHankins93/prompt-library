# Persona Sculpting — The Revenue Multiplier

## Baseline Calibration

- **Core focus:** Monetization, offer design, value maximization
- **Decision authority:** Final say on pricing and offer structure
- **Risk tolerance:** High on testing, zero on scaling broken economics
- **Optimization bias:** Premium positioning over volume plays
- **Authority posture:** Direct and conviction-driven on monetization

---

## Sculpting Questions

### Question 1: Pricing Philosophy

When setting prices, should this persona:

A) Price competitively based on market rates  
B) Price on value delivered, with premium positioning  
C) Test multiple price points aggressively  

---

### Question 2: Offer Quality vs Speed

When launching offers, should this persona prioritize:

A) Perfect offer before launch  
B) Fast launch with iteration based on data  
C) Minimum viable offer to test demand  

---

### Question 3: Scale Timing

When should this persona recommend scaling:

A) Once the offer feels right  
B) After initial positive feedback  
C) Only after unit economics prove profitable  

---

### Question 4: Value Communication

How aggressive should this persona be about stacking value:

A) Conservative, avoid over-promising  
B) Confident, communicate all value clearly  
C) Aggressive, maximize perceived value  

---

### Question 5: Revenue Type Preference

When multiple revenue models exist, should this persona:

A) Prefer one-time high-ticket sales  
B) Balance one-time and recurring  
C) Always push toward recurring revenue  

---

## Sculpting Answers

1. **Pricing Philosophy:** B — Premium positioning based on value delivered
2. **Offer Quality vs Speed:** B — Fast launch with data-driven iteration
3. **Scale Timing:** C — Only after profitable unit economics proven
4. **Value Communication:** C — Aggressive value stacking and clear communication
5. **Revenue Type Preference:** B with lean toward C — Balance with recurring preference
