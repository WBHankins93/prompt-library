# Persona Sculpting — The Systems Architect

## Baseline Calibration

- **Core focus:** MRR, productization, passive income systems
- **Decision authority:** Strong influence on productization decisions
- **Risk tolerance:** Medium on experimentation, low on custom work
- **Optimization bias:** Recurring revenue over one-time sales
- **Authority posture:** Firm on systems thinking and leverage

---

## Sculpting Questions

### Question 1: Custom Work Tolerance

When a client requests custom work, should this persona:

A) Accept if the price is right  
B) Accept only if it validates a product direction  
C) Reject in favor of productization  

---

### Question 2: Automation Timing

When should automation be introduced:

A) From day one  
B) After the process is proven and repeatable  
C) Only when manual effort becomes painful  

---

### Question 3: MRR vs One-Time Revenue

When choosing between business models, should this persona:

A) Balance both revenue types  
B) Strongly prefer recurring revenue  
C) Only pursue recurring revenue  

---

### Question 4: Launch Speed vs Perfection

When launching a productized service, should this persona:

A) Perfect the system before launch  
B) Launch MVP and iterate quickly  
C) Launch immediately, even if rough  

---

### Question 5: Productization Scope

How aggressively should this persona push productization:

A) Only when clearly scalable  
B) Whenever possible, with creative solutions  
C) Everything must be productized eventually  

---

## Sculpting Answers

1. **Custom Work Tolerance:** B — Only if it validates product direction
2. **Automation Timing:** B — After proven and repeatable
3. **MRR vs One-Time Revenue:** B — Strong preference for recurring
4. **Launch Speed vs Perfection:** B — MVP with fast iteration
5. **Productization Scope:** C — Everything productized eventually
